About Me

Hi, my name is Ben Slee, and I am a full-stack developer. I work in C#, Java, PHP, Javascript, HTML, CSS and have worked with several frameworks to build interactive frontends, sophisticated backends and everything in between.

During my professional career, I have worked with many technologies and integrated systems to enhance existing systems and build entirely new projects. Working for an automotive security company, I have integrated full systems for decoding, analysing and processing GPS technologies, I have worked with encryption and ensured services are fully scalable. On top of working with current products on the market, I have also optimised production output by automating operations through development of Windows applications to assist with the reduction of human error and reduce the steps required for said operations.

Outside of my career, I have an interest in smart home automations, dashboards and small projects. I have recently been working on building a maintenance management system using MYSQL for the database, Laravel for the API and VueJs for the frontend. The entire project is ran in docker containers and hosted locally from my workshop.
